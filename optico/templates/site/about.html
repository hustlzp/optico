{#

Product Highlights

#}

With 12 years of industry experience, Shenzhen Optico Communication Co., Ltd is a leading manufacturer of fiber optic components and a professional provider of FTTH and FTTA solution. Our production highlights:

Cable Assemblies MTP, MPO, SC, LC, FC, ST, MTRJ and other fiber patch cord, pigtail, adapters, attenuators and fast connectors;
Passive Components PLC Splitter, Fused coupler, CWDM, CCWDM, DWDM, AWG;
Active Products SFP transceiver, media converter, ONU device, OLT device, CATV transmitter
Cabling Solutions FTTH Solution, FTTX Solution, FTTA Solution, Fiber Cable, 5G Networks, Copper Cabling Solution, etc
